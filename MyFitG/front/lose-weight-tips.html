Weight Loss Tips

  • Maintain a calorie deficit by consuming fewer calories than you burn.
  • Incorporate high-protein meals to stay full longer and build lean muscle.
  • Drink plenty of water and avoid sugary beverages.
  • Get enough sleep to support recovery and prevent overeating.

Recommended Workouts

  • Cardio exercises like running, cycling, or swimming (30-45 minutes, 3-5 times a week).
  • High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) to burn fat efficiently.
  • Strength training to build muscle and boost metabolism.
  • Incorporate stretching and mobility exercises to avoid injuries.
