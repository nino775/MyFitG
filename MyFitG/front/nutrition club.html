Nutrition Recommendations

Explore our curated recipes for weight loss and muscle gain - all halal!

Breakfast

Protein-Packed Omelette

High-protein, low-calorie breakfast option.

Ingredients: Eggs, spinach, bell peppers

Instructions: Whisk eggs, cook with veggies, enjoy.

Greek Yogurt Parfait

Layered Greek yogurt with fresh berries and honey.

Ingredients: Greek yogurt, berries, honey

Instructions: Layer yogurt, berries, and honey.

Avocado Toast with Poached Egg

Whole-grain toast with avocado and poached egg for a protein and healthy fat boost.

Ingredients: Whole-grain bread, 1/2 avocado, 1 egg, salt, pepper

Instructions: Toast bread, spread mashed avocado, top with poached egg, season with salt and pepper.

Oatmeal with Berries and Nuts

A filling and nutritious oatmeal bowl topped with fresh berries and almonds.

Ingredients: 1 cup oats, 1/2 cup mixed berries, 1 tbsp almonds, honey (optional)

Instructions: Cook oats as directed, top with berries, almonds, and a drizzle of honey.

Banana Protein Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes made with bananas and protein powder for a balanced breakfast.

Ingredients: 1 banana, 1 egg, 1 scoop protein powder, cinnamon

Instructions: Mash banana, mix with egg and protein powder, cook pancakes on a non-stick skillet.

Chia Seed Pudding

A creamy and fiber-rich chia pudding made with almond milk and topped with fruit.

Ingredients: 1/4 cup chia seeds, 1 cup almond milk, 1 tsp honey, fruit topping

Instructions: Mix chia seeds and almond milk, refrigerate overnight, top with fruit and honey before serving.

Veggie and Cheese Omelette

A protein-rich omelette with bell peppers, onions, and cheese.

Ingredients: 2 eggs, 1/4 cup bell peppers, 1/4 cup onions, 1/4 cup shredded cheese, salt, pepper

Instructions: Whisk eggs, add chopped veggies, cook in skillet, sprinkle cheese before folding.

Smoked Salmon and Avocado Bagel

A flavorful bagel topped with smoked salmon, avocado, and herbs.

Ingredients: 1 whole-grain bagel, 1/4 avocado, 2 slices smoked salmon, fresh dill, lemon juice

Instructions: Toast bagel, spread avocado, add smoked salmon, garnish with dill and lemon juice.

Breakfast Burrito

A hearty wrap with scrambled eggs, black beans, and veggies.

Ingredients: 1 whole-wheat tortilla, 2 eggs, 1/4 cup black beans, 1/4 cup diced tomatoes, salsa

Instructions: Scramble eggs, warm tortilla, add eggs, beans, tomatoes, and salsa, roll and serve.

Egg White & Spinach Scramble with Turkey

A protein-packed scramble with egg whites, spinach, and lean turkey for a nutritious start to the day.

Ingredients: 4 egg whites, 1/2 cup spinach, 1/4 cup diced turkey breast (halal), salt, pepper

Instructions: In a non-stick pan, cook diced turkey until lightly browned, add spinach until wilted, pour in egg whites, and scramble until fully cooked. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Spicy Lentil Soup
